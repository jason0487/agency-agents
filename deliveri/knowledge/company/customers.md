# Deliveri Target Customers

Deliveri targets the 95% of e-commerce brands in the SMB category, primarily focusing on those who have achieved product-market fit but are operationally constrained.

## Primary ICP: SMB Consumer Brands
- **Revenue**: Sub-$2M annual revenue.
- **Platforms**: Shopify, TikTok Shop, Etsy, Instagram sellers.
- **Characteristics**:
    - Founder-led businesses.
    - Brand-sensitive: Deep concern for the customer post-purchase experience.
    - Limited operational resources.
    - Currently managing multiple logistics tools manually.

## Secondary ICP: Fulfillment Partners
- **Small Fulfillment Companies**: 3PL-lite providers looking for better logistics orchestration.
- **Mid-Market Brands**: Brands in the expansion phase requiring more robust systems.

## Key Personas
- **The Latin American + US Founder/Operator**: Entrepreneurs (25–40) looking for global reach and modern logistics.
- **The Operations Manager**: Tasked with reducing margin loss from logistics complexity.
- **The Customer Experience Lead**: Navigates the post-purchase journey and branding.

## Market Insight
- ~80% of e-commerce brands fail in their first year.
- **Logistics Complexity** is a primary driver of margin loss.
- The current ecosystem is a "patchwork" of point solutions (shipping, tracking, returns, tariffs all separate).
