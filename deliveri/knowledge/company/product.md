# Deliveri Product Knowledge Base

Deliveri is a "Logistics OS" for e-commerce, offering a unified control layer for post-purchase operations.

## Core Product Modules
### 1. Shipping & Labels
- **Multi-carrier rate shopping**: Integrated with USPS and UPS.
- **Label Generation**: High-speed generation with multi-service support.
- **Advanced Signature Options**:
    - Signature Confirmation
    - Adult Signature Required
    - Adult Signature Restricted Delivery
    - Signature Confirmation Restricted Delivery

### 2. Order Management
- **Centralized Dashboard**: Live lifecycle tracking.
- **Archiving with Recovery**: Intelligent banners with "Unarchive" CTA for better UX.

### 3. Tracking & Returns
- **Real-time Updates**: Real-time status for customers and brands.
- **Returns (Emerging)**: Return workflows and reverse logistics optimization.

### 4. Billing & Payments
- **Wallet System**: Prepaid credits to minimize per-transaction Stripe fees.
- **Auto-Recharge**: Continuous operations through automated funding.

## Courier Integrations
- **USPS**: Direct label generation with daily sweep billing (6:00 PM next day).
- **UPS**: Digital Access Partnership provider.
    - Full domestic service suite (Ground, 3 Day Select, 2nd Day Air, Next Day Air/Early/Saver).
    - High-value shipment workflows ($1000+ control logs).

## UX & Design Principles
- **Merchant-First**: Clean, transparent interface designed for founders.
- **Conditional UI**: Only show relevant options (e.g., Saturday delivery, pickup cancellation status).
- **Clear Messaging**: Proactive error handling (e.g., must cancel pickup before changing shipment).

## Strategic Roadmap
- **Returns Expansion**: Moving deeper into reverse logistics.
- **International Optimization**: Global shipping and tariff automation enhancements.
- **AI Automation Layer**: Embedding AI agents into each module for autonomous routing and support.
