# Deliveri Business Model

Deliveri builds the infrastructure layer for e-commerce logistics, starting with SMB brands and expanding into a global, AI-enabled logistics operating system.

## Revenue Streams
- **Margin on Shipping Rates**: Substantial discounts from UPS/USPS partnerships provide margin.
- **Per-Transaction Fees**: Labels and additional services.
- **SaaS Subscription**: Future and expanding revenue stream for platform access.

## Cost Structure
- **Carrier Costs**: USPS (immediate billing/next-day sweep), UPS (net terms).
- **Payment Processing**: Stripe fees.
- **Infrastructure**: AWS, external APIs, and technology services.

## Optimization Levers
- **Wallet System**: Reduces per-transaction fees by using prepaid credits (reduces Stripe overhead).
- **Carrier Partnerships**: Volume aggregation improves margins over time.

## Strategic Direction
- **Assistive First, Autonomous Later**: AI is embedded in workflows first to assist, then move toward autonomous decisioning (routing, cost, speed).
- **API-First Architecture**: Designed for both human operators and AI agents (MCP support).
- **Network Effects**: Moving toward an embedded logistics layer across the commerce ecosystem.
