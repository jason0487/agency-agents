# Deliveri Company Overview

Deliveri is an end-to-end e-commerce logistics platform built for fast-growing SMB consumer brands. It simplifies and unifies the post-purchase experience by integrating shipping, tracking, returns, tariffs, and customer engagement into a single system.

Deliveri does NOT operate warehouses or handle physical inventory. Instead, it acts as a logistics orchestration layer across carriers, fulfillment partners, and software systems.

## Mission
Deliveri exists to simplify global logistics for modern commerce by giving merchants clarity, control, and operational leverage.

## Vision
Deliveri aims to become the default logistics layer for modern commerce, powering both human and AI-driven operations globally. Become the "Operating System for Logistics" used by fast-growing consumer brands.

## Value Proposition
- **Reduce Costs**: Up to ~90% savings via carrier partnerships.
- **Unified Control**: Eliminate fragmented logistics tooling.
- **Enterprise Capabilities for SMBs**: Access to advanced logistics features for sub-$2M revenue brands.
- **Strategic Advantage**: Turn logistics from a cost center into a competitive edge.

## Strategic Narrative
1.  **Big Change**: E-commerce logistics has become fragmented and inefficient.
2.  **Problem**: Brands juggle multiple disconnected tools.
3.  **Solution**: Deliveri unifies logistics into one platform.
4.  **Outcome**: Lower costs, faster delivery, and a better customer experience.
