# Deliveri Operational Model

Deliveri operates as a logistics orchestration layer, integrating carriers, fulfillment partners, and internal business systems.

## Strategic Partnerships
- **Carrier Partners**:
    - **UPS**: Digital Access Program provider (strategic).
    - **USPS**: Direct label and billing integration.
- **Go-To-Market Partners**:
    - **Seguno**: Primary Shopify ecosystem partner.
- **Advisors**:
    - Alex Tsai (Alibaba).
    - Heidi Isern (ex-Narvar).

## Internal Business Systems
- **HubSpot**:
    - **Customer Support**: Integrated through help@ondeliveri.com.
    - **Marketing & Transactional Emails**: Unified messaging across the lifecycle.
- **Gleap**:
    - **Customer Support Automation**: Gleap chatbot for Dash (AI-assisted support).
    - **Documentation**: Gleap Help Center is the primary repository for customer-facing knowledge.

## Operational Constraints
- **Cash Flow Sensitivity**: USPS bills next-day (6:00 PM sweep); UPS operates on 14-day net terms.
- **API Limitations**: Reliability depends on carrier API performance and uptime.
- **Financial Compliance**: Strict reconciliation required between carrier bills and merchant wallet usage.

## Known Risks
- **Carrier Dependency**: High reliance on USPS and UPS for services.
- **Margin Pressure**: Competitive pricing requires continuous carrier negotiation.
- **Operational Complexity**: Scaling to thousands of brands requires advanced automation (hence the move toward AI agents).

## Long-Term Ambition
Deliveri aims to be the **Operating System (OS)** that bridges e-commerce platforms and physical fulfillment globally.
