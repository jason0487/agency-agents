# Deliveri Brand Identity

Deliveri presents itself as a modern, forward-thinking logistics operating system for e-commerce.

## Brand Personality
- **Modern**: Latest technology and tech-forward approach (AI-native).
- **Global**: Connected across carriers and countries.
- **Empathic**: Understands the struggles of founder-led SMBs.
- **Reliable**: Consistent SLAs and trusted carrier partnerships.
- **Innovative**: Building the future of autonomous logistics.

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Latin American and US e-commerce entrepreneurs (25–40).

## Visual Identity
### Typography
- **Logo**: Gelica (modern, friendly, legible).
- **Headlines**: General Sans / Roboto Serif.
- **Body**: Satoshi.

### Colors
- **Primary**: Yellow (vibrant, energy, speed).
- **Accent**: Dark Gray (professionalism, stability).
- **Secondary**: Dark Yellow and Off White (warmth and clarity).

## Core Messaging
> "Deliveri is an end-to-end e-commerce logistics platform built for fast-growing consumer brands."
